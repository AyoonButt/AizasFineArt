Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about commissioning artwork, purchasing, and working with me.

Standard commissions typically take 6-8 weeks from initial consultation to completion. This includes time for planning, sketches, approval, and the painting process. Rush orders (2-4 weeks) are available for an additional fee, while extended timelines (10-12 weeks) may be preferred for larger or more complex pieces.

Commission prices vary based on size, medium, and complexity. Watercolor pieces typically start at $300 for smaller works (8x10"), while oil paintings start at $500. Larger pieces and complex compositions are priced accordingly. I always provide a detailed quote before beginning any work, and a 50% deposit is required to start.
