Why I Choose Different Mediums

Each medium speaks its own artistic language, and I select the one that best expresses the soul and story of each subject I paint.

Watercolor

Gentle & Flowing

Perfect for...

  • Delicate florals and botanical subjects
  • Capturing the essence of light and atmosphere
  • Creating gentle, meditative compositions
  • Expressing spontaneous, emotional moments
View Watercolor Works

Oil Paint

Bold & Expressive

Perfect for...

  • Complex landscapes with rich detail
  • Portraits requiring depth and dimension
  • Works needing strong color saturation
  • Classical, timeless artistic statements
View Oil Paintings
Each medium tells its own story. Watercolor whispers with gentle transparency, while oil paint speaks with bold, confident strokes. I choose the medium that best serves the emotional truth of each subject.
